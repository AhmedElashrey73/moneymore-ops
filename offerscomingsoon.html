Coming Soon Offers
A new offer type for offers that aren't yet live but are about to be. Users can opt in to a "Notify me when live" push that bypasses the standard discovery-push rules and fires the moment the offer activates. Designed to capture intent before launch and convert it to a claim within minutes of go-live.
The problem
The current Offers tab only shows live offers. If we know a partner is launching in 3 days, we can either show nothing and lose the build-up, or show it and let users feel disappointed they can't claim. Coming Soon offers solve this by giving the user a clear path: see the upcoming offer, opt in for a notification, and get a push the moment it goes live so they're first in line.

Plural agreement: "Starts in {N} days" follows the same four-case rule as the main brief's "{N} days left" countdown. Same for hours, if a coming-soon offer drops within 24h of go-live.
Accessibility — what changes what you draw
  • Coming Soon and Notifying-you states need text labels alongside the colour change. Purple gradient on the hero, purple coupon block on the card — accessibility relies on the explicit "Coming Soon" / "🔔 Notifying you" text plus 🔔 / ⏳ icons, not the colour alone.
  • State transition (opted in) needs an announcement. Screen reader should announce "Notifications enabled" or equivalent when the user opts in — the visual change alone isn't enough.
  • Confirm dialog focus. Default focus on "Keep notifying me" (safer action), not the destructive option.
  • Touch targets 44×44px on the notify-me CTA, the cancel CTA, and the confirm dialog actions.
  • Contrast on the purple hero. White text on the purple gradient (#5B3A8E middle stop) — verify 4.5:1, especially for the small "Starts in 3 days" pill text against the gradient.
