Budget Creation
Budgets tab — setting a monthly spending limit by category
Problem
Users who import their bank SMS have no way to set a spending limit. Without a budget, the app shows what happened but cannot warn the user before they overspend. Budget creators retain at significantly higher rates — this is the highest-value feature in the restructure.
