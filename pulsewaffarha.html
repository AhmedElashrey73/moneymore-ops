C Customer Intelligence

A verified window into your actual customers.

Separately from the category view, MoneyMore can identify a seed of confirmed Waffarha customers — people whose Waffarha payment surfaced in bank SMS — and then profile their full spending behaviour across the open economy. Small today, designed to deepen as coverage grows.

~1,000
confirmed customers identified
We detect roughly EGP 500K of Waffarha-attributable payments in bank SMS. That figure isn't the prize — it's the key. It lets us identify a verified seed of people we know are your customers, then read their entire lifestyle wallet with MoneyMore's full view. The value is everything else these confirmed customers do — not what they spent with Waffarha.
!
Read this as directional. This seed sits right at our 1,000-user k-anonymity floor, and it's a biased slice — skewed toward customers whose Waffarha payment left a bank-SMS trace, not a random sample of your base. So these are whole-cohort patterns, not drill-downs: we deliberately don't slice this group into sub-segments, because that would fall below the floor and manufacture noise. It's a proof of method that sharpens as coverage grows.
