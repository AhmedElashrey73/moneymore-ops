Budgets Tab
A dedicated tab where users see, create, edit, and delete budgets. The tab opens in one of four states depending on the user's data: empty, recommendation, hybrid, or list. Recommendations come from spending averages and are permanently dismissible.
The problem
Users with imported transactions can see what they spent but have no way to set a limit. Without budgets, the app is a record-keeper, not a spending tool. The tab needs to do two things at once: make the value of budgeting obvious for users with no budgets yet, and make ongoing budget management feel light for users who already have them.

Accessibility — what changes what you draw
  • Touch targets — 44×44px minimum on every interactive element. Includes the recommendation card "Set budget" CTA, dismiss link, status badges (if tappable), category picker rows, detail sheet action rows, and the centre plus button.
  • Contrast — 4.5:1 minimum for text, 7:1 for financial figures (limits, spent amounts, remaining values). Verify across white card surface and the cream recommendation surface.
  • Never colour-only for state. Budget status badges combine colour with text label. Progress bars combine colour with badge text. Disabled action sheet rows combine reduced opacity with the explanatory sub-line.
  • Focus order — banner → recommendations → budget list → bottom nav. Logical top-to-bottom on the tab. On Arabic the visual flips to RTL but focus order stays top-to-bottom. Inside the detail sheet: limit field → stats → change category → delete.
  • Status badge width must accommodate "Close to limit" (14 chars) at standard density. If badge truncation occurs, fall back to "Close" / "قرب" before going single-letter or icon-only.
